Minecraft

Markus "Notch" Persson started working on Minecraft in 2009 as a hobby project inspired by games like Dwarf Fortress and Infiniminer. The game was originally called "Cave Game" and then "Minecraft: Order of the Stone" before settling on its final name, which is a combination of the words "mine" and "craft".

Stardew Valley

Stardew Valley was created by game designer Eric Barone, under the alias of ConcernedApe. During the four years he spent solo-developing it, he taught himself the necessary skills to produce the game's music, art, programming, and design.
